Posole

Description

This is a recipe for Posole Rojo, an authentic Mexican pork and hominy stew.

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz dried guajillo and ancho chiles
  • 1 108 oz can of white hominy
  • 3 pounds pork shoulder
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano

Steps:

  1. Boil 5 quarts of water.
  2. Heat the chiles, covered in 3 cups of water.
  3. Brown the pork in a pan with olive oil and garlic.
  4. In a large pot of boiling water, add the pork and spices.
  5. Prepare the red sauce
  6. Add the red chili sauce into the pot with the pork and hominy.
  7. Cook for 2-3 hours until the pork is tender.
